## Takeaways

- ZKsync chains after the Atlas upgrade are **fully EVM-equivalent**, meaning you can write smart contracts in Solidity or Vyper as in
  Ethereum and test apps with standard EVM tooling.

- You can use ZKsync's latest testnet, the **ZKsync OS Developer Preview**, to test your apps as you develop them.

- As you continue building with ZKsync,
make sure to join our [GitHub Discussions Community](%%zk_git_repo_zksync-developers%%/discussions/) to
  ask questions, get help and help other devs building on ZKsync, or share your project.
