## Prerequisites

Before you move onto the next section, make sure that you:

1. Have a browser wallet extension installed.
  If you're not sure what wallet to use, try [Metamask](https://metamask.io/).
1. Have configured the %%zk_ostestnet_name%% testnet in your wallet.
1. Have bridged at least 0.05 testnet ETH to your wallet on the %%zk_ostestnet_name%% network.
