## What are crosschain asset transfers?

Crosschain asset transfers mean that you can send any asset directly from one ZKsync chain to another ZKsync chain without using a bridge.
They will be available on mainnet chains that settle on ZKsync Gateway after the `v31` upgrade.

## How is it different than bridging?

With ZKsync Connect crosschain asset transfers,
assets are natively tracked across ZKsync chains.
Instead of locking an asset when bridged, it is fully burned on Chain A before getting minted on Chain B.

Assets are tracked on each L2 chain through asset tracker contracts, which are deployed on the L1, Gateway, and all L2 chains.
The asset transfer contracts ensure that no chain is able to transfer more assets than it has.
