Prividium™ is a commercial product built on the open-source [ZK Stack](/zk-stack/license). While the underlying
ZK Stack is freely available, Prividium™ components require a commercial license.

## Commercial License Required

Prividium™ extends the ZK Stack with proprietary components for permissioned chains:

* Proxy RPC with authentication enforcement
* Permissions API and Admin Panel
* Private Block Explorer
* Prividium™ SDK

These components are provided exclusively to licensed organizations. Access requires a commercial agreement with Matter Labs.

::callout{icon="i-heroicons-exclamation-triangle" color="amber"}
Prividium™ components are not open source. Unauthorized use, distribution, or modification is prohibited.
::

## Obtaining a License

1. **Contact Matter Labs** to discuss your requirements.
1. **Review the license agreement** and commercial terms.
1. **Receive access** to Prividium™ components, documentation, and support.

For licensing inquiries, contact the Matter Labs team at [prividium@matterlabs.dev](mailto:prividium@matterlabs.dev).
